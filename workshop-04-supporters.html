Session 04: Finding the Right Supporters

Once your project is clear, the next question is not “How do I get funding?” but “Who would actually care about this?”

Not every funder is the right funder. Not every donor is aligned with every project. The goal is not to ask everyone—it is to find the people, organizations, or institutions already aligned with the kind of work you are doing.

When alignment is strong, conversations become easier. Your project makes sense to them because it fits what they already value, support, or invest in.

This is where AI can help significantly. It can help you:

  • Identify types of donors and funders
  • Find organizations supporting similar work
  • Generate lists of possible partners
  • Suggest where your project fits

But AI is a starting point—not a substitute for real relationships. It can help you find doors, but you still have to walk through them.

Discussion Points

  • Who is already supporting work like yours?
  • What types of funders would naturally align with your project?
  • What would make someone say, “This fits what we care about”?
  • What relationships do you already have that could connect to this?

Prompt to Try

Based on my project, help me identify potential supporters, including donors, organizations, foundations, and partners. Suggest categories and specific examples I could research or reach out to.
