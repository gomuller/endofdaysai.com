Session 05: Turning Ideas Into Materials

Once your project is clear, AI can help you turn that clarity into useful materials. This is where a rough idea begins to become something you can share, send, print, present, or build from.

Many people get stuck because they know what they want to do, but they do not yet have the documents that help others understand it. They need a project summary, a donor letter, a grant paragraph, an outreach email, a budget explanation, or talking points for a meeting.

AI can help create first drafts of these materials quickly. The goal is not to let AI speak instead of you. The goal is to use AI to get past the blank page, organize your thoughts, and create something you can revise with your own judgment.

A first draft is not the final truth. It is a starting place.

Used well, AI can help people who do not see themselves as writers communicate with more confidence. It can make funding work feel less intimidating and more possible.

Discussion Points

  • What materials would help your project move forward?
  • Do you need a one-page summary, donor letter, grant draft, or outreach email?
  • What parts would be easiest for AI to help draft?
  • What parts still need your personal voice and judgment?

Prompt to Try

Based on my project, help me create a first draft of the materials I need to seek support. Start with a one-page project summary, then suggest a donor letter, outreach email, and short spoken pitch.
