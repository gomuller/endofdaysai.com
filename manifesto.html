The Signal

AI is not just another technology wave. It is a force multiplier for thought, labor, creativity, surveillance, persuasion, automation, and power.

The old world was built around scarcity: scarce knowledge, scarce expertise, scarce distribution, scarce production.

That world is ending.

End of Days AI exists to track what comes next: the tools, the disruptions, the risks, the opportunities, and the strange new systems forming in real time.

This is not panic. This is preparation.

The signal is live.
