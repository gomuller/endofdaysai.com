Session 10: Next Steps After the Workshop

You now have something many people never reach: clarity about a meaningful project, a defined audience, a working story, potential supporters, and a first set of materials you can use.

The next step is not to wait. It is to act—simply, directly, and consistently.

Immediate Next Steps

  • Refine your one-page summary
  • Identify 3–5 people or organizations to contact
  • Send your first outreach message
  • Continue clarifying your project with AI as needed

You do not need everything finished before you begin. Momentum comes from taking the next clear step, not from waiting for perfection.

AI can continue to help you refine, organize, and communicate—but it is your responsibility to act, follow up, build relationships, and carry the work forward.

Discussion Points

  • What is the next concrete step you will take after today?
  • Who is one person you will reach out to?
  • What part of your project still needs clarity?
  • How will you continue using AI wisely moving forward?

Prompt to Try

Help me plan my next 7 days for moving this project forward. Break it into simple, realistic steps including refining materials, identifying contacts, and making initial outreach.

Final Thought

AI is a tool. Clarity is a discipline. Action is a choice.

The future of your project will not be decided by the technology— but by what you do next.
